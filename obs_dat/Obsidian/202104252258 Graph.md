# General
##### TYPES: 
 - TB (From Top to Bottom)
 - LR (From Left to Right)
 - BT (From Bottom to Top)


|Length	| 1	|2		|3	|
|---	|----|---|---|
|Normal	|`---`|	`----`	|`-----`	|
|Normal with arrow	| `-->`	|`--->`	|`---->`	|
|Thick	| `===`	|`====`	|`=====`	|
|Thick with arrow	| `==>`	|`===>`	|`====>`	|
|Dotted	| `-.-`	|`-..-`	|`-...-`	|
|Dotted with arrow| `-.->`	|`-..->`	|`-...->`	|
